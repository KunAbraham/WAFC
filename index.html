West Africa Consultants (WAfCon)

The West Africa Consultants (WAfCon) for Peace, Security and Development is dedicated to advancing knowledge, fostering innovation, promoting peace, and building stronger communities. Through our research, training, conflict resolution, and cooperation initiatives, we strive to create a more harmonious and prosperous world.

Services We Offer

Research and Evaluation

Are you looking for an evidence-based approach to your work, implementing your projects, and evaluating your approach?

Look no further! WAfCon supports all aspects of social science research from designing research projects and writing funding proposals to data collection, data analysis, interpretation, and report writing.

Training in Research and Evaluation

Does your organization wish to integrate research into program design, implementation, and evaluation? Do you want to strengthen your research capacities and improve your research activities?

WAfCon provides research training that is tailored to your needs. Our research training is hands-on. We provide on-the-job training for research projects.

Learn More

Alternative Dispute Resolution

Are you experiencing a conflict in your organization, in your community, between community and the government or other entities?

Alternative dispute resolution processes like mediation, arbitration, and traditional practices are effective methods to address a wide range of conflicts. At WAfCon, we provide bespoke services to help transform your conflicts and find sustainable solutions for a peaceful coexistence.
